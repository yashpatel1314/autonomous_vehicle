Maze Design

5 horizontal corridor lanes (3 cells wide each) separated by 1-cell-thick walls. The robot serpentines through all 5 lanes.

68 obstacle cells form the 4 wall rows. With inflation_radius=1 the planner treats cells adjacent to walls as blocked — leaving a 1-cell passable centerline through each corridor.

Gate checkpoints sit at the lane-switching connectors — where the wall ends create a natural pinch point the robot must pass through to continue. The robot drives through the green sphere and continues to the next gate.
